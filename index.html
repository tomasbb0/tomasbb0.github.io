About

Meet Burnay Labs.

Burnay Labs is a strategic holding company based in Lisbon, Portugal. We operate across two pillars: management consulting that drives measurable business transformation, and technology ventures built and shipped in house.

On the consulting side, we partner with founders and executive teams on strategy, go to market execution, and operating design, turning complex business challenges into clear action plans. On the technology side, we design, engineer, and launch products across AI platforms, SaaS tools, and consumer apps.

Burnay Labs Education is our language tutoring platform for young learners worldwide. We are also building the next generation of AI powered business automation through our OpenClaw agent framework.
