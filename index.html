About

Hi, I'm Tomás.

I'm a builder based in Lisbon, Portugal. I design, develop, and ship software products end-to-end, from idea to production. My stack is whatever gets the job done fastest: vanilla JS for speed, Python for AI, Firebase for backend, Chrome APIs for extensions.

When I'm not coding, I'm teaching Portuguese to kids around the world through The Burnay Labs Education platform, or exploring AI-powered automation workflows.
